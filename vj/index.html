Why VJ CARS?

VJ CARS is a ISO 9001 : 2015 Certified Pre-owned Multi Brand Car Showroom in Saidapet, Chennai. We have built a reputation for quality and service. We pride ourselves on offering our customers - responsive, competent and effortless ownership experience, hence provide one-stop solutions like clear pre&post sale documentation, personalised vehicle finance, insurance etc., to our clients with regards to hassle-free car ownership.


Our Flagship Showroom is spread across multiple floors with a display area of over 20000 sqft and it is your gateway to exclusive vehicle demonstrations and in-depth experiences with our impeccably well maintained pre-owned cars, which look and feel as good as new. All our cars are pre-checked and certified by a team of expert engineers to give our clients a worry free buying experience.


The exceptional quality of our carefully curated pre-owned cars are an envy to ones eyes and embodies the echoes of 'Not what a car can do. Rather, what a car can do for you’. We strongly believe that a relationship with a client is one that starts, rather than ends, with your purchase at VJ CARS.
